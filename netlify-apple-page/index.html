Moodot

Privacy & Support

Moodot is a minimalist mood journal that lets you save one color a day, write short notes, and view your personal patterns over time.

Privacy Policy Support

Privacy Policy

Moodot is designed to keep the core journaling experience simple and local-first. Your daily entries and settings are stored on your device. If you choose to connect a premium cloud account, selected data may also be synced securely with Firebase to restore your journal across devices.

What we collect

  • Daily mood entries you create, including selected color and optional note.
  • App preferences such as palette selection, reminders, and premium status.
  • Optional cloud account information if you enable premium sync.

How data is used

  • To save and display your mood journal inside the app.
  • To sync your journal across devices if you enable cloud backup.
  • To manage premium access, optional exports, and optional rewarded ad unlocks.

Advertising and purchases

Moodot may use AdMob for optional rewarded ads and RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. No interstitial ads are shown in the main journaling flow.

Third-party services

  • Firebase for authentication and optional cloud sync.
  • RevenueCat for purchase management.
  • Google AdMob for optional rewarded ads.
  • Apple and Google platform services for in-app purchases and sign-in.

Your choices

  • You can use the core app without creating an account.
  • You can disable reminders in the app settings.
  • You can disconnect cloud sync at any time.
  • Premium users can request cloud account deletion from within the app.

Children's privacy

Moodot is not intended for children under 13. If you believe personal data was provided by a child, contact support and we will review the request.

Policy updates

This page may be updated from time to time to reflect product or legal changes. Continued use of Moodot after updates means the revised policy applies.
